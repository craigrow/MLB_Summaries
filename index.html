Andrew Painter showcased his talent on the mound, earning the win with five solid innings, while Kyle Schwarber and Adolis García provided the power with back-to-back homers that set the tone early.

Despite a late rally from the Nationals, highlighted by Daylen Lile's three-hit performance, the Phillies held on thanks to Jhoan Duran's save. A fielding error by first baseman Luis García Jr. in the fifth allowed the Phillies to stretch their lead to three, a cushion that ultimately proved vital as the Nationals clawed back in the later innings.
